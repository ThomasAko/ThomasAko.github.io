About us

Our journey started 2 years ago from a casual conversation among friends about possibilities for increased international trade between Cameroon and Belgium. Why Cameroon and Belgium ? The answer is actually quite simple - unlikely but potentially explosive growth. These countries are not typically associated with each other. Belgium only receives 5% of Cameroon's exports. On the other hand, 7% of Cameroon's exports come from Belgium. We see lots of room for improvement . . .

Our mission : increase business transactions and interactions between Cameroon and Belgium (and the EU eventually).

Our vision : To become the leading Belgium-based private sector actor for trade in natural products and technology services from Cameroon.
