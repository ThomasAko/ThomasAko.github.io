What are our core values?

We believe in harnessing the power of business networks to overcome business challenges. The power of such a network is strongly dependent on the quality of the connections between its members. That is why we chose to include the word connections in our name. That is why we focus on hand-picking companies which are compatible with our core values; trust, action and knowledge.

Trust
Trust is important because people typically do business with partners they trust; strong relationships breed trust.
Action
Action is at the core of every successful business. Businesses which are proactive anticipate trends before everyone else, getting the timing of key moves in the market place right every time.
Knowledge
Knowledge is the cornerstone of every business's operations. In a technology-driven world, technical knowledge makes it possible to take full advantage of strong relationships and ensures you deliver first class quality.

We believe every business is unique. As you go about developing awesome products, creating value for your clients and growing your business further you face diverse challenges related to your particular line of business. TAK connections exists to help you extend your network and find unique connections which make a difference for your business. We are Europe based (working out of our HQ in Belgium) but crucially, we have an excellent feeling for and understanding of the international business landscape. Whether you are based in Africa, Asia or America, we can put our European network and connections at your disposal.

We have an expanding network of connections in Europe which can be activated at any time to fulfil your business needs. The relationships we nurture with businesses based in Europe are the cornerstone of our activities. We work with you to find solutions which are adapted to your own home market situation. Get in touch with us by phone or e-mail, we may just have what you are looking for.
