| Why we exist

Most businesses love to focus on serving their clients. Depending on your specific industry, finding the right balance between being cost effective, using internal resources efficiently and providing maximum value for your clients can be tricky. For a lot of companies, critical parts of their business process cannot be found locally. They need to import products from abroad in order to fulfil their business objectives. However, finding the right export partner abroad can be challenging. Finding export partners based in Europe can be challenging - you may lack knowledge of the Belgian market, travelling in person to Belgium may be impractical, you are on a tight budget etc.

This is where we come in, we offer companies the possibility to import raw materials, electrical and mechanical equipment, chemicals, and other critical items from Belgium. We speak English, French and Dutch. Additionally, we are comfortable with the latest technologies. Our exclusive focus on suppliers and export partners based in Belgium enables us to offer a customized service which is not only efficient but also tremendously effective.

| What we do

We work with you to solve your most important sourcing/import problems. Our typical client is a company interested in importing raw materials, equipment or chemicals from Belgium. We are your eyes and ears on the ground. Our bottom line is dependent on your satisfaction, so we will always have your best interests in mind when interacting with potential sourcing partners.

We work with you to identify the main challenges in your import process. We then develop an importing strategy and waste no time in rolling it out.
