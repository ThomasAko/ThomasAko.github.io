So how does it work ?

New and refurbished EU phones and tablets

We prefer to sell in bulk (several) devices. However, we also do retail sales. Get in touch by WhatsApp (+32 491 46 46 91) or e-mail (sales@takconnections) to place your order. We always advice our customers to get a precise idea of the specification of the phone(s) and/or tablet(s) they wish to buy. You can check the specification of any phone here. 

These days phones are becoming more and more expensive. Coupled to the need for sustainability and reuse of resources, we think it is worthwhile (and usually cheaper) to reuse lightly used devices. Our team of engineers makes sure the refurbished phones are fully functional an drun on the latest software.

After accepting your order, we issue a proforma invoice. In addition to the cost of your devices, the proforma invoice also includes shipping costs. All our prices are quoted in Euros (€) and are exclusive of value added tax (VAT). If you are buying from outside Europe, you don't have to worry about VAT.
