Services

Our services fall into three broad categories; sales expansion, business development and procurement. Each of these have a are very broad scope. You can find some specific details about each category below.

Procurement

Do you need specific equipment and/or services for your day-to-day operations, new projects or may-be you have just been awarded a new contract and you need an external partner to help out with supply contracts. We have industry expertise in electrical and mechanical engineering. We serve the following industries telecom, medical, power and oil & gas.

Business Development

Work with a trusted partner on new business opportunities. We connect you with other businesses whose activities are complementary to yours. Are you looking for partners for joint ventures, are you an Africa-based company that is looking for European partners for your projects, we can connect you with several interesting companies to help you take your business to the next level.

Sales Expansion

Increase your revenue by gaining access to new markets or diversifying your offering in markets where you are already present. Are you a European manufacturer who is considering expanding your product offering into hitherto unknown locales in central or west Africa? Is you company thinking about expanding sales activities to the Europe? We can help you set things up.
