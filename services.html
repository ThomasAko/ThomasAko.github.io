Services

We help manufacturers and engineering companies based in Europe grow their revenues by expanding their sales operations abroad. Our services are tailored for three main client groups. First, sales expansion and representation - for European manufacturers wishing to expand their sales activities. Second, procurement engineering - for companies typically based outside Europe who are looking to acquire mechanical or electrical equipment (parts or assemblies) from Europe-based suppliers. Third, business development - for companies intersted in working on high-value projects in Africa. You can find some specific details about each category below.

Sales Expansion & Representation

Increase your revenue by gaining access to new markets or diversifying your offering in markets where you are already present. Are you a European manufacturer who is considering expanding your product offering to new regions? Is you company thinking about expanding sales activities to Europe? We can help you set things up.

Procurement Engineering

Using our excellent technical expertise, we specialise in supplying a wide range of mechanical and electrical equipment (parts, assemblies, devices) from renowned manufacturers. Do you need specific equipment for your day-to-day operations or may-be you have just been awarded a new contract and you need an external partner to help you out. We serve the following industries telecom, medical, power and oil & gas.

Business Development

Work with a trusted partner on new business opportunities. We connect you with other businesses whose activities are complementary to yours. Are you looking for partners for joint ventures, are you for example an Africa-based company that is looking for European partners for your projects, we can connect you with several interesting companies to help you take your business to the next level. We also help European companies interested in increasing their revenues by working on projects abroad.
